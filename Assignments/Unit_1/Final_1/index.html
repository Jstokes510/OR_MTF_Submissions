Cultured In Oakland Ca

Origin

Hood Certified is a Collective of 2 artists from Oakland california. Their main goal is to make quality art from real oakland culture. We are trying to keep the culture of our art alive.

Oakland

A lot of the art behind Hood Certified has derived from the struggle and pains of growing up in east oakland but also the greatness that comes out of it.

Pop Up Shop

Now Hood Certified Makes community art and has a pop up shop that is showing up to community events
